Why Did I Start This Business?

Audio and video has always been a passionate hobby of mine even since I was little. I remember playing with my keyboard and tinkering with movie-makers and aways enthroled to see fancy production equipment. Years later now, the thrill still hasn't worn off. Whether it's seeing a new camera, mixer, or amp. I'm always happy to learn and try something new. But why trust me?

I have built home theaters for myself and others. I have studied audio and video inorder to understand the hardware and the physics involved. I have done everything from live streaming events to setting up home theaters and car audio. Now I'm looking to take that hobby and passion of mine and use it for Northwestern Montana.
